Leslie Cheung

a Hong Kong singer and actor

Here's a time line of Leslie Cheung's life:

  • 1956 - Born in HongKong, China
  • 1976 -Begin to pursue a career in show business.
  • 1984 - Achieved wide popularity with his self-titled album and its single "Monica", whose upbeat dance production introduced a new popular trend to Cantopop in addition to the contemporary pool of sentimental ballads.
  • 1987 - Release best-seller Summer Romance album.
  • 1988 - Won most Popular Male Artist.
  • 1989 - Won Jade Solid Gold Best Ten Music Awards.
  • 1990 - Played a womaniser longing for the return of his estranged mother in Days of Being Wild.
  • 1991 - Won him Best Actor at the 1991 Hong Kong Film Awards.
  • 1993 - His role as a homosexual Peking opera actor in Farewell My Concubinecatapulted him to prominence in the western world.
  • 1997 - Cheung's reputation as a queer celebrity consolidated with his role in the 1997 drama Happy Together, a film explicitly depicting a homosexual male relationship.
  • 1999 - Awarded the Golden Needle Award, the highest distinction of the RTHK Top 10 Gold Songs Awards.
  • 2000 - Honoured as "Asia's Biggest Superstar" at the CCTV/MTV Music Honours in mainland China.
  • 2003 - Died by suicide by jumping off the 24th floor of the hotel Mandarin Oriental.
